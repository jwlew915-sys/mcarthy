Platform

Sean's Priorities

Built on Responsibility, Respect, and Communication — the three principles that guided 33 years of public service.

Priority 01

Government That Answers You

A top-to-bottom review of North Hempstead's long-dormant Master Plan with measurable goals for revenue, land use, and infrastructure. Real responses — no runarounds.

Priority 02

Building Code Reform

A comprehensive audit of commercial and residential regulations to streamline approvals, cut months of delays, and save residents and businesses thousands of dollars.

Priority 03

Senior Independence

Expanding Project Independence with more recreational programs, transportation support, and employer partnerships so every senior can age in place with dignity.

Priority 04

Fiscal Transparency

Restoring full transparency to the town budget process. Every dollar tracked, every expenditure justified, every investment tied directly to community outcomes.

Meet the Candidate

A Life of
Service

Sean McCarthy is a 33-year veteran of local law enforcement and a lifelong North Hempstead resident. He rose through the ranks of the Nassau County Police Department to serve as Chief of Support Services.

In 2024, Sean was elected Police Commissioner of the Port Washington Police District — proof that his neighbors trust him to lead. Now he's bringing that same accountable, transparent leadership to Town Hall.

Sean and his wife Lisa have three children and have called Port Washington home their entire lives. He knows these streets, these schools, and these families personally.
